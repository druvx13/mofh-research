MyOwnFreeHost & iFastNet Research

An In-Depth Analysis of iFastNet's Free Reseller Hosting Service

Read Research Key Findings

Overview

MyOwnFreeHost (MOFH) represents a unique offering within the web hosting landscape, providing individuals with the opportunity to establish their own free web hosting companies. Launched in 2007 by Byet Internet, a company with a substantial history in the web hosting sector, MOFH operates as a reseller service under the umbrella of its parent company, iFastNet.1

The core principle behind MOFH is to equip users with the necessary infrastructure and tools to offer free hosting services under their own brand name, primarily targeting those who wish to enter the hosting market without significant initial investment.1 While this model presents an attractive entry point, a comprehensive understanding of MOFH requires examining its features, the role of iFastNet, user experiences, pricing structures, legal frameworks, and its position relative to other hosting providers.

Key Insight: MOFH is not an independent entity but rather a reseller service offered by iFastNet.2 Discussions and community support related to MOFH can often be found on platforms like the Byet.net forum.5

Established History

The long-standing presence of Byet Internet, dating back to 2007, implies considerable experience in the free hosting domain, which could translate to a degree of reliability.

Community Support

The existence of a dedicated forum like Byet.net fosters a sense of community among users and resellers, offering a valuable avenue for support and information exchange.

Key Features

Reseller Benefits

For individuals choosing to become resellers through MyOwnFreeHost, the platform offers a compelling "unlimited package." This includes unlimited disk space, bandwidth, hosted domains, and MySQL databases for the reseller's overall operation.1 This abundance of resources at the reseller level aims to remove concerns about limitations as their hosting business grows.

White-Labeling

A significant advantage of MOFH is its white-labeling capability, which allows resellers to fully brand the system with their own company name and domain.

Advertising Integration

MOFH provides for resellers to incorporate their own advertising code, such as Google Adsense, into the web pages and control panels of their free hosting clients.

Support System

MOFH includes a free, white-labeled support system that enables clients to directly contact the reseller for assistance.

iFastNet Infrastructure

The underlying infrastructure and support for MyOwnFreeHost are provided by its parent company, iFastNet.2 iFastNet offers a diverse range of premium hosting services, including options like Super Premium, Ultimate Premium, VPS Servers, and Dedicated Servers, catering to users with more demanding needs.6

Important Note: The reliability and effectiveness of MyOwnFreeHost are intrinsically linked to the quality of infrastructure and support provided by iFastNet, making iFastNet's reputation a key factor in evaluating MOFH.

Premium Features

  • High-speed SSD servers
  • Generous bandwidth allocations
  • Complimentary SSL certificates
  • Free domain registrations
  • Integrated CloudFlare support

Business Model

By offering both a free reseller platform (MOFH) and a range of premium hosting options, iFastNet employs a tiered business strategy aimed at attracting a broad spectrum of users, from those prioritizing zero cost to those requiring enhanced performance and features.

User Experiences

To gain a balanced perspective on MyOwnFreeHost, it is essential to consider the experiences and opinions of its users, gathered from various online platforms such as the Byet.net and InfinityFree forums3, Reddit4, and dedicated review websites.10 Notably, InfinityFree stands out as a popular free hosting provider that utilizes MyOwnFreeHost's reseller service, indicating the reach and influence of the MOFH platform within the free hosting community.3

Positive Feedback

"The unlimited resources for resellers make it easy to start a hosting business without worrying about limits."

"The white-label feature is excellent for creating a professional hosting brand."

Negative Feedback

"Support response times can be slow during peak periods."

"Some users report unexpected account suspensions for resource usage."

Market Position: In the competitive landscape of web hosting, MyOwnFreeHost occupies a unique position as a provider of free reseller hosting. When comparing its free plan with other direct free web hosting providers like InfinityFree, GoogieHost, Freehostia, FreeHosting, and 000webhost3, certain distinctions emerge in terms of features and limitations.

Pricing Structure

A key aspect of MyOwnFreeHost is its pricing structure, particularly the fact that it allows users to launch a web hosting company without any initial financial outlay.1 The free plan for resellers comes with an impressive set of "unlimited" features, including unlimited reseller accounts, disk space, bandwidth, databases, and domains.1

Reseller Benefits

  • No upfront costs to become a reseller
  • 5% commission on paid hosting upgrades
  • Ability to integrate own advertising
  • White-label branding options

End-User Limitations

  • 5 GB disk space (configurable by reseller)
  • 100 GB monthly bandwidth
  • Maximum of 400 databases
  • Up to 50 domains hosted

Important Consideration: The "free" nature of MOFH primarily pertains to the reseller account itself. While resellers can offer free hosting to their clients, there might be associated costs or restrictions for those free accounts, such as the potential need for a custom domain or limitations on resources.

Legal Framework

The legal framework governing the use of MyOwnFreeHost is outlined in its Terms of Service (TOS), which is provided by iFastNet as the reseller service provider. The most current version of the TOS can be accessed at http://byethost.com/legal.php.2

Key TOS Provisions

  • Prohibition of unlawful, harmful, or infringing content
  • Policy against excessive resource usage
  • Age restrictions (generally 16+)
  • Limit of three free hosting accounts per individual

Privacy Policy Highlights

  • Collection of personal data including names, addresses, and contact information
  • Data security measures implementation
  • User rights to access, update, or delete personal information
  • Information sharing with trusted third parties for services like payment processing

Conclusion

In conclusion, MyOwnFreeHost, a reseller service provided by iFastNet, offers a distinctive platform for individuals to launch their own free web hosting companies. It provides resellers with an attractive "unlimited" resource package and white-labeling capabilities, facilitating entry into the hosting market without upfront costs. However, potential users, both those seeking to become resellers and individuals looking for free web hosting, should be aware of the potential limitations that often accompany free services.

Advantages

  • No upfront costs for resellers
  • Comprehensive white-label branding
  • Monetization through advertising and commissions
  • Established infrastructure from iFastNet

Considerations

  • Potential resource limitations for end-users
  • Variable support quality
  • Strict terms of service enforcement
  • Dependence on iFastNet's infrastructure

Final Recommendation: For those interested in starting a free reseller hosting business, MOFH presents a compelling opportunity with its comprehensive feature set. However, individuals seeking free web hosting should research resellers utilizing the MOFH platform and carefully consider the potential trade-offs compared to direct free hosting providers or paid alternatives. A thorough review of the terms of service and privacy policies is strongly recommended.
