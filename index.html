MyOwnFreeHost & iFastNet Research

An In-Depth Analysis of iFastNet's Free Reseller Hosting Service

Read Research Key Findings

Overview

MyOwnFreeHost (MOFH) represents a unique offering within the web hosting landscape, providing individuals with the opportunity to establish their own free web hosting companies. Launched in 2007 by Byet Internet, a company with a substantial history in the web hosting sector, MOFH operates as a reseller service under the umbrella of its parent company, iFastNet.

The core principle behind MOFH is to equip users with the necessary infrastructure and tools to offer free hosting services under their own brand name, primarily targeting those who wish to enter the hosting market without significant initial investment.

Key Insight: MOFH is not an independent entity but rather a reseller service offered by iFastNet. Discussions and community support related to MOFH can often be found on platforms like the Byet.net forum.

Established History

The long-standing presence of Byet Internet, dating back to 2007, implies considerable experience in the free hosting domain, which could translate to a degree of reliability.

Community Support

The existence of a dedicated forum like Byet.net fosters a sense of community among users and resellers, offering a valuable avenue for support and information exchange.

Key Features

Reseller Benefits

For individuals choosing to become resellers through MyOwnFreeHost, the platform offers a compelling "unlimited package." This includes unlimited disk space, bandwidth, hosted domains, and MySQL databases for the reseller's overall operation.

White-Labeling

MOFH offers white-labeling capabilities, allowing resellers to brand the system with their own company name and domain. However, resellers must display iFastNet promotion on their websites and include iFastNet upgrade offers in the control panel.

Advertising Integration

While MOFH allows resellers to incorporate advertising code like Google AdSense, there are significant limitations. AdSense requires per-domain approval and ads.txt file setup, which the testcookie system may interfere with. Additionally, injecting ad code into user websites risks account termination if ads appear on prohibited content.⁹

Support System

MOFH includes a free, white-labeled support system that enables clients to directly contact the reseller for assistance.

iFastNet Infrastructure

The underlying infrastructure and support for MyOwnFreeHost are provided by its parent company, iFastNet. iFastNet offers a diverse range of premium hosting services, including options like Super Premium, Ultimate Premium, VPS Servers, and Dedicated Servers, catering to users with more demanding needs.

Important Note: The reliability and effectiveness of MyOwnFreeHost are intrinsically linked to the quality of infrastructure and support provided by iFastNet, making iFastNet's reputation a key factor in evaluating MOFH.

Premium Features

  • SSD storage (now standard across most hosting providers)⁹
  • Generous bandwidth allocations
  • Complimentary SSL certificates
  • Free domain registrations
  • Note: CloudFlare cPanel integration and Railgun are no longer available⁹

Business Model

The only reason MOFH is free is that it helps bring paying customers to iFastNet. By offering both a free reseller platform (MOFH) and a range of premium hosting options, iFastNet employs a tiered business strategy aimed at attracting a broad spectrum of users.

User Experiences

To gain a balanced perspective on MyOwnFreeHost, it is essential to consider the experiences and opinions of its users, gathered from various online platforms such as the Byet.net and InfinityFree forums, Reddit, and dedicated review websites.

Positive Feedback

"The unlimited resources for resellers make it easy to start a hosting business without worrying about limits."

"The white-label feature is excellent for creating a professional hosting brand."

Negative Feedback

"Support response times can be slow during peak periods."

"Some users report unexpected account suspensions for resource usage."

Market Position: In the competitive landscape of web hosting, MyOwnFreeHost occupies a unique position as a provider of free reseller hosting. When comparing its free plan with other direct free web hosting providers, certain distinctions emerge in terms of features and limitations.

Pricing Structure

A key aspect of MyOwnFreeHost is its pricing structure, particularly the fact that it allows users to launch a web hosting company without any initial financial outlay. The free plan for resellers comes with an impressive set of "unlimited" features.

Reseller Benefits

  • No upfront costs to become a reseller
  • 5% commission on paid hosting upgrades
  • Ability to integrate own advertising
  • White-label branding options

End-User Limitations

  • 5 GB disk space (configurable by reseller)
  • No enforced bandwidth limits in practice⁹
  • Maximum of 400 databases
  • No limit on addon domains (only 50 subdomains limit)⁹

Important Consideration: The "free" nature of MOFH primarily pertains to the reseller account itself. While resellers can offer free hosting to their clients, there might be associated costs or restrictions for those free accounts.

Legal Framework

The legal framework governing the use of MyOwnFreeHost is outlined in its Terms of Service (TOS), which is provided by iFastNet as the reseller service provider. The most current version of the TOS can be accessed at http://byethost.com/legal.php.

Key TOS Provisions

  • Prohibition of unlawful, harmful, or infringing content
  • Policy against excessive resource usage
  • Age restrictions (generally 16+)
  • Limit of three free hosting accounts per individual

Privacy Policy Highlights

  • Collection of personal data including names, addresses, and contact information
  • Data security measures implementation
  • User rights to access, update, or delete personal information
  • Information sharing with trusted third parties for services like payment processing

Conclusion

In conclusion, MyOwnFreeHost, a reseller service provided by iFastNet, offers a distinctive platform for individuals to launch their own free web hosting companies. It provides resellers with an attractive "unlimited" resource package and white-labeling capabilities, facilitating entry into the hosting market without upfront costs.

Advantages

  • No upfront costs for resellers
  • Comprehensive white-label branding
  • Monetization through advertising and commissions
  • Established infrastructure from iFastNet

Considerations

  • Requirement to display iFastNet promotions
  • Practical limitations with advertising integration
  • Variable support quality
  • Dependence on iFastNet's infrastructure

Final Recommendation: For those interested in starting a free reseller hosting business, MOFH presents a compelling opportunity with its comprehensive feature set. However, individuals seeking free web hosting should research resellers utilizing the MOFH platform and carefully consider the potential trade-offs compared to direct free hosting providers or paid alternatives.
